Freefall! is a simple game in which you are in a space ship falling through a pit. Your goal is to survive as long as possible, and score as many points as you can. Multipliers (*) accelerate your score gains, the obstacles (ø) set your multiplier to 1.

Frequently Asked Questions

I can't get back to the game from the High Scores list

    The standard back gesture works, it is just a little bit awkward in landscape orientation. Just turn the phone into it's normal portrait orientation, and use the back gesture.
How can I pause the game?
    Tapping anywhere on the screen will pause the game.
Why does the game randomly pause?
    The game detects when the phone lags for any reason & automatically pauses the game to minimize its affect on gameplay. If this happens often for no apparent reason, you should restart your phone.
How can I get back to the difficulty selection screen?
    Tapping the title bar will bring up that screen as well as pause the game
